the egyption museum

the egyption museum

One of the most important landmarks of Cairo tourist and even Egypt in general; it is one of the largest museums space and holdings, the museum contains about 150 thousand pieces about the history of Egypt Pharaonic, Roman and Greek

Cost of admission to the Egyptian Museum:LE 20 (for Egyptians) / LE 160 for other nationalities .

Cost of admission to the Egyptian Museum at night: LE 30 (for Egyptians) / 220 LE for other nationalities.

Working hours of the Official Egyptian Museum: 9 am - 5 pm Sunday and Thursday 9am - 9pm

The Coptic Museum

The Coptic Museum

The Coptic Museum is located next to the famous Babylonian Roman fortress, the largest museum of Egyptian Coptic antiquities in the world, where you will see the most important artifacts dating back to the Coptic era of Egyptian history, which number about 16,000 artifacts from different regions of Egypt.

Cost of admission to the Coptic Museum: 10 LE (for Egyptians) / 100 LE for other nationalities

Working hours of the Coptic Museum Official: 9 am - 5 pm

The Museum of Islamic Art

The Museum of Islamic Art

The Museum of Islamic Art is located in the Bab al-Khalk area, one of the most important tourist attractions in Cairo and one of the largest Islamic museums in the world that mimics the era of Islamic rule in Egypt.

Cost of admission to the Museum of Islamic Art: LE 10 (for Egyptians) / LE 100 for other nationalities

Opening hours of the official Islamic Art Museum: 9 am - 5 pm

The largest mosque in Cairo. The high-rise Ibn Tulun Mosque features its famous minaret on its roof where you can climb up and enjoy the air frescoes and see the old Cairo from above.

Al-Azhar Mosque or Al-Azhar Mosque is one of Cairo's most important religious landmarks not only at the level of worship and spirituality but at the level of the study of forensic sciences and the world over a thousand years.

The ancient mosque, which dates back to the Fatimid era, has a distinctive architectural design that combines two styles.
